OUR STORY

Flor’s Canteen started in 2004, when Flordeliza Sarmiento opened a small food business to serve affordable, home-cooked meals to the community. With simple dishes and a focus on quality, it quickly became a favorite spot for students, workers, and nearby residents. In its early years, the canteen became known for its lutong-bahay meals—warm, familiar, and budget-friendly. Customers often say that the food tastes different in a good way—unique compared to other nearby canteens. Because of this, many keep coming back and recommend it to others. Now, after 22 years since it first opened, Flor’s Canteen continues to thrive. From 2004 up to now, it remains a simple yet well-loved canteen, known for its unique flavors and loyal customers that span generations.

MISSION

To provide the community with delicious, high-quality, and affordable home-cooked meals that bring warmth to every customer.

VISION

To be the leading provider of authentic Filipino comfort food, recognized for our commitment to quality, affordability, and community spirit.
